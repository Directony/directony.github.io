About Me

Heyo, I'm an Iowa based screenwriter & graphic designer. While I don't have much professional experience in either field, I've been able to build up my works with personal projects. Gotta start somewhere.

When it comes to my writing, I have a passion for creating emotional moments. I enjoy writing stories people can connect to. As far a genres, I like to explore any genre that sounds interesting. I am currently working on a sci-fi/horror feature, and have plenty of ideas for future film and short film scripts.

As for my graphic design projects, I tend to gravitate towards logo design and recreating retro style printed materials. Like anyone, I do have my shortcomings, but I can always find a good solution for anything I can't do.

Take a look at what I've made so far and let me know if you'd like to work with me!
